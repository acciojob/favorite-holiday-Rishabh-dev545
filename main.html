Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its romantic atmosphere, rich history, art, and culture, Paris offers something unforgettable for every traveler.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – a relaxing way to see Paris by night.
  5. Champs-Élysées – a world-famous avenue perfect for shopping and dining.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – flaky and buttery French pastries.
  • Baguette – the traditional French bread enjoyed fresh every day.
  • Macarons – colorful, delicate, and sweet treats.
  • Escargot – a classic French delicacy of snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Crème Brûlée – a creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar top.
